Who are we?

Since 1962, Choco-Latte has been crafting premium chocolates that bring joy, warmth, and the perfect balance of flavors to chocolate lovers everywhere. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful idea — to blend the richness of fine chocolate with the comforting, aromatic allure of coffee. Today, we’re proud to carry on that vision, delighting customers with luxurious confections made from the highest-quality ingredients. At our core, we are a family of chocolatiers, with each generation contributing to the artistry and legacy of Choco-Latte. From the very first chocolate bars created in our kitchen to the innovative truffles and chocolate blends we offer today, each product carries the signature of our founders’ vision and the care of the team who follows it. We believe in the importance of hands-on craftsmanship, and it’s this dedication to quality that has built Choco-Latte into the brand it is today.While we’ve grown over the years, we still hold dear the values of passion, integrity, and creativity that drove us from the very beginning. Every member of the Choco-Latte team shares in the belief that chocolate is more than just a product—it’s an art form. Our chocolatiers, from our master creators to the apprentices learning the trade, are all dedicated to making every piece of chocolate a masterpiece.

Our Story

Founded in 1962, Choco-Latte started as a small family-owned chocolate shop with a passion for quality and a commitment to innovation. Over the years, we’ve grown from a humble storefront to a beloved brand known for its rich, handcrafted chocolates and unique flavor blends. What began as a single recipe has now evolved into a wide range of products, each one inspired by our founders' love of both chocolate and coffee. Founded in 1962 by a family of passionate chocolatiers, Choco-Latte started as a small chocolate shop in the heart of our town, where we served hand-made confections made from carefully sourced cocoa beans. With a deep love for the rich taste of coffee and the art of chocolate making, our founders sought to create something extraordinary—a unique fusion of premium chocolate and freshly roasted coffee. What started as a dream in a modest kitchen has grown into a beloved brand that now serves chocolate enthusiasts across the world.

A Sweet Legacy

With more than 60 years of expertise, we’ve had the privilege of sharing our chocolates with generations of customers. Whether you’re a long-time fan or a newcomer to the Choco-Latte experience, we invite you to discover why we remain a beloved name in the world of premium chocolates. Thank you for being a part of Choco-Latte's rich history. We look forward to many more years of creating moments of sweetness, warmth, and joy for chocolate lovers everywhere. Here’s to savoring the finest chocolate — since 1962.
